How it works

Click to add or remove walls. Drag the start or finish markers to reposition them. Press Visualize to animate the search.

A* uses a heuristic to explore efficiently. Dijkstra is optimal but exhaustive. BFS finds the shortest path on unweighted grids. DFS dives deep and is not guaranteed to find the best path.

Change the grid size to explore dense or wide-open layouts. Random walls quickly generate fresh mazes.
